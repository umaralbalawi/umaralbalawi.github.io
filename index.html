BIOGRAPHY

Umar Albalawi received the bachelor’s degree in computer science in 2006, and the master’s degree in computer science from Texas A&M University-Commerce, in 2013. He also got the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from University of North Texas in 2016. He worked as Teacher Assistant at the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology, University of Tabuk, from 2007 to 2010. He was a Research Assistant with the Department of Computer Science, Texas A&M University Commerce, in 2013. He has published 16 peer-reviewed research articles. He is currently the Vice-Dean Faculty of Computers & Information Technology and the department chair of Computer Engineering.
